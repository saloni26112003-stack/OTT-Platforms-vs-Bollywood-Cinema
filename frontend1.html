OTT vs Bollywood Comparison

  • Accessibility: OTT platforms allow anytime streaming, while Bollywood requires theatre visit.
  • Cost: OTT subscriptions are cheaper; Bollywood cinema tickets are high.
  • Content Variety: OTT provides web series, movies, documentaries; Bollywood focuses mostly on movies.
  • Audience Reach: OTT is global; Bollywood depends heavily on regional release.
  • Growth: OTT revenue rising faster compared to Bollywood post-pandemic.
